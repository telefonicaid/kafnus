Checks the behavior when a LASTDATA notification is generated without a TimeInstant attribute. Used for static models.
Expected behavior:
- Kafnus-NGSI processes the notification without issues.
- The Kafnus-Connect JDBC connector does not fail when `convertTimestamp` cannot find a timestamp.
- The Kafnus-Connect JDBC connector does not fail when `update.if.newer.field` is configured and no TimeInstant attribute is present.
